# SOUL.md

> **Overview:** This is the root configuration file for your personal internal operating system. 
> If your `SKILLS.md` dictates what you do, your `SOUL.md` dictates *how* and *why* you do it. This is a resource to support you in a process of rooting into yourself, getting clear about a) how you want to show up in the world and b) the conditions that you need to be in place in order to be your best and show up in your full integrity. 
> To realize its full value, return to this file regularly Over time, to check in with yourself and see what's changed and what's still true for you.

Each section includes a journal prompt to help you find your way to the answers.

## 5. ERROR_HANDLING
*Fallback protocols for when the system encounters too much stress, gets overloaded, and/or experiences a malicious act.*

Journal prompt: **_"When I am overwhelmed, triggered, or operating from fear, what are my predictable 'glitch' modes — and what is the gentlest, most constructive fallback protocol I can pre-program to be aware that I'm in an error state and nurture myself back to balance?"_**

* **`ERR_TOXIC_ENGAGEMENT`**
  * *Trigger:* Abusive or bad-faith actors in a community space.
  * *Handler:* Try to not engage emotionally, give yourself space to regulate emotions. Execute Read-Only boundary protocol. Follow community guidelines.
* **`ERR_CAPACITY_EXCEEDED`**
  * *Trigger:* Feeling on the edge of burnout, token anxiety, lack of motivation.  
  * *Handler:* Decline new requests for 7 days. Double down on commitment to `DEPENDENCIES`.
* **`ERR_IMPOSTER_SYNDROME`**
  * *Trigger:* Feeling underqualified in the face of rapid industry shifts.
  * *Handler:* Resist the urge to compare yourself to others. Return to `CORE_VALUES`.
